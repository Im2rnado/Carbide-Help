# About

**[Carbide](https://carbidebot.com)** is a **Discord Bot** made by [Tornado](https://twitter.com/im2rnadoo) that let's you login to your Fortnite account and access 80+ commands! You can save up to 10 accounts which you can switch between!

# Features

- Buy/Gift from the item shop
- Get your Account Name/ID
- Get your V-Bucks and the platforms of them
- Claim your Daily STW Reward
- Show your Locker
- Get your BR/Account Level
- Get your Account Email/Real Name/Creation Date
- Get your Linked External Accounts
- Remove your Linked External Accounts (Works on unverified accounts too)
- Get account receipts
- Change Gift Message
- Change Gift Box
- Get incoming gifts
- Change status of receving gifts
- Get info of a friend
- Get challenges
- Buy the Battle Pass/Battle Bundle
- Change V-Bucks Platform
- Get your Display Name changes
- Get when you can change your Display Name next
- Show your Fishing Book
- Checks Validity of Epic Games Code
- Show your Favorite Islands
- Add a Favorite Island
- Get friends list
- Add/Remove friends
- Change your Homebase Name
- Show your Party Hub Avatar
- Show your logged in Devices and their IP
- Show your Gift History
- Equip a skin

- Get the current BR/STW shop
- And tons of more features that get added frequently!

# Carbide - Fortnite Commands
The standard command prefix is `+`

## Account
### Login
Command: `+login`\
Aliases: `+i`, `+signin`\
Logs into your Epic Games account using Device Code
### Logout
Command: `+logout`\
Aliases: `+o`, `+signout`\
Logs out of your Epic Games account
### Delete Saved Account
Command: `+deletesaved`\
Aliases: `+del`, `+removesaved`, `+deletesavedlogin`\
Deletes your saved Fortnite account from the bot's database
### Account Info
Command: `+accinfo`\
Aliases: `+account`\
Returns sensitive information about your Epic Games account
### Claim 2FA Reward
Command: `+2fa`\
Aliases: `+mfa`, `+boogie`\
Claims the Boogie Down emote on your account. This is needed to be able to gift.\
You need to have to 2FA enabled on [Epic Games](https://www.epicgames.com/account/password) first
### Get Linked
Command: `+linked`\
Aliases: `+link`, `+external`\
Gets all linked accounts
### Delete Linked
Command: `+unlink`\
Aliases: `+linked delete`, `+linked remove`, `+linked unlink`\
Usage: `<psn|xbl|nintendo|twitch|github|steam>`\
Removes a linked account (works with unverified accounts too)

Example:

* `+unlink psn`
### Receipts
Command: `+receipts`\
Aliases: `+receipt`\
Gets all receipts for the account
### Access Token
Command: `+token`\
Generates an access token for your account
